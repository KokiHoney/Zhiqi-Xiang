Summary

The project I made this time is a website about fitness. Users can log in to the website to record their daily fitness status and check-in. You can record your daily fitness plan and process, and formulate your own fitness plan to urge users to complete their goals. At the same time, there is an icon on the webpage, which can record their own weight curve and system curve, and users can clearly observe their own changing trends after recording their own data. In addition, the corresponding videos on the website are also provided for users' reference, which can help them find their goals easily and quickly. At the same time, you can communicate with others on the website, communicate with each other and answer questions. The website can also be accessed on a variety of devices because of its responsive design practices.

Purpose

"Health is only one step away from you"

Targent audience

This software is very suitable for the following users The first is for beginners who have just chosen fitness. They can use this website to formulate a clear plan for themselves, so that they can do fitness activities regularly. If they don't know which project is right for them, they can communicate with others through videos on the website or forums to find a project that suits them best. The second is the group of people who do not have strong restraint on themselves, but want to lose weight very much. More and more young people choose to exercise to make their body more beautiful, but they often do not have the efficiency and plan to complete it. own goals. This website can help them set a plan over a period of time to help them do these exercises on a regular basis. The second is the obese people. Everyone wants to have this beautiful figure, not only for good looks, but also for health. More and more people are getting fat and not getting enough exercise to get them moving. This website can solve their problems. They can not only find the corresponding videos on the website to follow at home, but also can discuss fitness problems and share their experiences with other users in the discussion area on the website after the gym workout.
